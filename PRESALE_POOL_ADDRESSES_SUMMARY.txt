===============================================
HOW THE ROTATION WORKS:
===============================================

1. When a pre-sale token is created, the system assigns
   the wallet with the lowest current volume that isn't full

2. Each purchase adds to that wallet's volume counter

3. When a wallet reaches 20,000 sats, it's marked as "full"
   and the next wallet in rotation is used

4. You can monitor wallet status via:
   GET http://localhost:3002/api/presale/pool-stats

5. To reset or adjust, edit:
   token-indexer/src/config/presale-pool.ts
